About

What started as a way to keep her mind busy during the pandemic, has become a full-blown passion. Gina started Late Night Knots in 2020 and has since immensely enjoyed creating lovely crochet items for her customers. She hopes that the joy she feels when she creates each piece is transferred to the people who she creates for. Gina loves being able to provide quality handmade items that range from accessories to stuffed animals.
